A Heart wrapped in fur

The biard or berger de brie, is a large bread of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The bredd was used by the Frenc Army as sentries, messengers adn to search for wounded soliders bacause of its fine sense of hearding. Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson adn Lafayette all owned briards
